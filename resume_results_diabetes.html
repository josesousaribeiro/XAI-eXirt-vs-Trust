Passo 1

Performance Stability Analysis

Figure 1 presents the pairwise p-values derived from the Friedman-Nemenyi test, comparing the performance metrics reported in Table \ref{tab_performance}. Within the comparison matrix, lower p-values indicate stronger evidence to reject the null hypothesis of performance equivalence. Adopting a significance threshold of $\alpha = 0.05$, we can identify statistically significant differences with 95\% confidence.
